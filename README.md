# Supermarket Sales Analysis (Egypt)

## Overview

This project demonstrates a simple and practical sales analysis dashboard designed for small supermarket owners.

The goal is to provide clear insights about:

* Best-selling products
* Sales trends over time
* Category performance
* Price impact on sales

---

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel (Dashboard for business users)
* Power BI (Interactive version for analysis)

---

## Key Insights

* Some products generate high revenue but low volume.
* Some products sell frequently but contribute less to total sales.
* Sales fluctuate monthly, showing clear peak and low periods.
* Categories perform differently and require different strategies.

---

## Dashboard Features

### KPIs:

* Total Sales
* Total Units Sold
* Average Basket Value
* Average Units per Basket

### Visuals:

* Top Products by Sales
* Top Products by Quantity
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Category Distribution
* average price over time
---

## Business Value

This dashboard helps supermarket owners to:

* Identify high-performing products
* Avoid stock shortages on fast-moving items
* Understand pricing impact on demand
* Improve product mix decisions
